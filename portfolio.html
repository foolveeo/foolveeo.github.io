Bachelor Thesis: Multi-Temporal SAR Images Analysis for Seasonal Rice Crop Estimation

This classifier was developed as bachelour thesis work during an intership at Sarmap, a Swiss company in the remote sensing field. The scope of the internship was to develop an algorithm capable of detecting temporal information about rice field crops. From a series of satellite images a map is created, depicting the date of the start of the rice season and how many times a crop was yielded inside the period analyzed.
